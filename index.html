Projects

The commercial airline industry is the foundation of our global transportation system. In 2012 approximately 3 billion passengers travelled by plane. The entire industry generates more than $800bn in revenue each year. The US airline industry accounts for $190bn of that. Airlines have seen massive optimizations. Despite this, passengers are often left frustrated as their flights are delayed. According to the Bureau of Transportation Statistics (BTS) about 20% of all flights are delayed. A flight is considered delayed by the BTS if its arrival or departure time is 15 minutes later than scheduled. In 2007 these delays cost the US economy approximately $32.9bn. Poor weather conditions are one of the main causes for these delays.

My aim is to create a ML model that can accurately predict whether a flight will be delayed. I would also like to be able to predict how long it will be delayed for. I will do this by creating a ML model using historical flight data and historical weather data. In addition to this I would like to create a front end where a user can check my model’s prediction on their planned flight. As you can see I am currently in the Data Acquisition & Formatting stage.



Crime in America has fallen over the last quarter century, with exceptions such as rape and hate crimes. Despite this, the public believes that it is rising. This could be due to the increasing amount of information that we take in via technology.

I analysed data on crime in Boston using Python. In the few years of data that I have I tried to see if Boston follows the same trends and if not, why?

I aimed to find distribution patterns in our data and whether or not socioeconomic factors cause them. I also looked at the relationships between our variables and try to find correlations which will help me answer questions about the development of crime in Boston.
